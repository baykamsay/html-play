How To Make a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

  1. Get the peanut butter, jelly, and 2 pieces of bread.
  2. Spread the peanut butter on the bottom bread.
  3. Spread the jelly on the top bread.
  4. Merge the 2 pieces of bread.
